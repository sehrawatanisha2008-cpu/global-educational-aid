A platform to help students find verified international scholarships

About the Platform

Global Educational Aid is an independent student-built platform created to help learners identify genuine international scholarships and funding opportunities from official sources.

Purpose and Impact

Many students struggle to find reliable scholarship information due to misinformation, paid agents, and unverified websites. This platform aims to reduce that confusion by listing only official and trusted programs.

  • Verified government and university scholarships
  • No paid promotions or third-party agents
  • Focus on merit-based and fully funded opportunities

The platform is designed and maintained independently with the goal of making scholarship discovery simpler and more transparent for students.
